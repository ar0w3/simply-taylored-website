Our Story

Simply Taylored LLC is your go-to source for creating unforgettable moments. From custom party decorations and themed cookies to mobile craft parties, I help bring your vision to life with a personal touch.

Founded by Taylor Rowe, Simply Taylored LLC was born out of a passion for creativity, celebration, and making every event special. I believe that every detail matters, and my mission is to provide high-quality, fun, and unique experiences for families and communities alike.

I am dedicated to designing personalized parties, crafting delicious treats, and ensuring that every event runs smoothly from start to finish. I love what I do, and it shows in every smile, every decoration, and every cookie I deliver.

Thank you for considering Simply Taylored LLC. I can’t wait to help you create your next memorable celebration!
